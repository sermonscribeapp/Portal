Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Recording & Transcription

SermonScribe uses Apple's Speech Recognition framework, which is generally very accurate for clear English speech. Accuracy may vary with heavy accents, background noise, or very fast speakers. You can always edit the transcript after recording.

Subscription & Billing

The free version includes:

  • 5 recordings per month
  • Basic transcription
  • Manual note editing
  • Text export

SermonScribe Pro unlocks:

  • Unlimited recordings
  • AI-powered summaries
  • Automatic action item extraction
  • Full-text search across all notes
  • PDF export
  • 7-day free trial to try everything

Subscriptions are managed through Apple:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find SermonScribe and tap Cancel Subscription

You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Open SermonScribe on your new device and go to Settings. Tap "Restore Purchases" and your Pro subscription will be restored automatically, as long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

Privacy & Data

Open any note and tap the share button. You can export as text (free) or PDF (Pro). Share to any app on your device: Notes, Files, email, AirDrop, or anywhere else.

You can delete individual recordings by swiping left on them in the notes list. To delete all data, remove the app from your device. Since we don't store any of your data on our servers, deletion is immediate and complete.

Sermons delivered in public church services are generally considered public speech and can be recorded for personal use in most jurisdictions. However, laws vary by location. We recommend checking local recording consent laws and informing speakers when possible. SermonScribe is intended for personal note-taking, not redistribution.
